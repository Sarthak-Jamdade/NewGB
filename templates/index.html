A Poem I Could Not Keep Inside

❝
I did not know a corridor could feel like home, until I found you standing in it.
We built a world in borrowed classrooms, in canteen queues and hurried notes, in the silence between lectures that somehow always filled with us.
Today the gates will close behind us and college will become a photograph — but you, my love, you will never become a thing of the past.
So let this last walk be slow. Let my hand find yours one more time in these familiar, beloved halls. Because endings, when shared with you, still feel like the most beautiful thing.
— yours, always, beyond every last day 🌸
